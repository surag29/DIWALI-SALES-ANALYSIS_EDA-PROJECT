## ğŸ“ˆ Key Findings

### Customer Demographics
- **Gender Insight:** Female customers represent the majority of buyers with significantly higher purchasing power (74.3M) compared to males (31.9M)
- **Age Group:** The 26-35 age group dominates with 42.6M in sales, followed by 36-45 and 18-25 age groups
- **Marital Status:** Married women show the highest purchasing power, particularly in the 26-35 age bracket

### Geographic Distribution
- **Top States:** Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka lead in both order volume and sales value
- **Regional Concentration:** Southern and Western zones show strong market presence

### Occupational Patterns
- **Top Sectors:** IT, Healthcare, and Aviation professionals are primary buyers
- **Professional Demographics:** Salaried professionals from high-earning sectors contribute significantly to sales

### Product Categories
- **Best Sellers:** Food, Clothing & Apparel, and Electronics & Gadgets dominate sales
- **Category Performance:** These three categories account for the majority of transactions during the festival

### Target Customer Profile
**Primary Target:** Married women, age 26-35, from UP/Maharashtra/Karnataka, working in IT/Healthcare/Aviation sectors, purchasing from Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories.
